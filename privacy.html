Privacy Policy for “Homework Bro Bro”

Effective date: December 6, 2025

We keep this policy short, honest, and easy to read.
If anything is unclear, reach out and we’ll explain it in plain words.

1. What we collect and why

Data we collect How we get it Why we need it How long we keep it
Browser user-agent string, screen resolution, page URL, and timestamp Client-side JavaScript that POSTs once per 30-minute session to a private Discord webhook Anonymous abuse-prevention and bot detection Kept only in the Discord message history; no server-side logs retained

We do not collect:

  • Your name, email, or any account info (there are no accounts)
  • Exact location, GPS, or precise geolocation
  • Any personal files or messages
  • Data from children under 13 (the site is general audience, not directed at kids)

2. Cookies & tracking

  • First-party only
  • One tiny cookie/localStorage item for dark/light mode
  • Google Analytics (IP anonymized) — block it with uBlock Origin or any ad-blocker
  • No marketing cookies, no Facebook pixel, no third-party trackers

3. Ads

We show Google AdSense ads. Google may set its own cookies and collect data according to its own privacy policy.
Opt out of personalized ads here: https://adssettings.google.com

4. Data security

We use HTTPS and basic rate-limiting to keep the site safe. No sensitive data is stored.
